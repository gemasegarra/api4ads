To filter ads by name: http://localhost:3000/

?name=name

To filter ads by price: http://localhost:3000/

?price=number

. i.e. http://localhost:3000/

?price=20

. You can also filter ads by min and max price:

?price=-20

will return all ads with a price of 10 or lesser,

?price=20-

will return all ads with a price of 10 or greater and

?price=20-30

all ads with a price between 20-30
To filter ads by tags: http://localhost:3000/

?tags=tag

(lifestyle, mobile, motor, work) i.e. http://localhost:3000/

?tags=lifestyle
